    • The majority of the game will be set in Hell where you start out being just an ordinary guy/girl that knows how fight a bit, but realize you must do whatever it takes in order to save the overworld from being taken over by demons. At first, demons will come back to fight you; however, eventually you’ll learn how to kill them for good. As you fight to the end, you come across many different tiers of demons such as Archdemons where the fight may take longer or prove more challenging. Eventually, you may even have to take on the Devil himself, but that depends on how the story is played out. Two alternate endings so far is to lock yourself in hell to keep the demons from ever invading again, or returning to the overworld and continue the fight there while looking for a solution to stop the demons from coming back.
